Starting With Scala (draft)

Scala is a functional programming langugage. For years, I’ve always shied away from functional programming languages. I’ve done that becuase I always thought they were only really used by hard-core academic computer scientists to make themselves sound intelligent in front of others. I’ve found out recently there are some really fun and powerful things to be learned from functional languages, such as Scala. They’re fast, good for asynchronous operations, have low latency and because of that they are becoming more prevelant and sought after to meet the real-time, low-latency demands of today.
